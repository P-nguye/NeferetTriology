## Characters Page
Highly popular section with readers. Includes descriptions, visual design notes, and background information:

### Main Characters
* **John Carter:** Age 24. Dark brown hair, athletic build, historian/adventurer look. 
* **Jack Reynolds:** Slightly broader build, short light-brown or sandy hair, engineer/inventor vibe. Often wearing practical outdoor clothing or technical gear.
* **Dr. Ethan Mitchell:** Late 50s, distinguished professor, wears glasses, thoughtful scientist.
* **Princess Neferet:** Egyptian royal beauty, intelligent and graceful.
* **Ethan Blake:** Successful executive, polished, confident, charismatic.

### The Royal Family of Neferet
* **Pharaoh Khufu (The King / Father):** Mid-50s. Powerful ruler overseeing construction of the Great Pyramid. Commands respect throughout the kingdom. Loves Neferet deeply but struggles with balancing family and duty. Initially suspicious of John Carter, but eventually recognizes John's honor and courage. *Personality:* Wise, Strategic, Authoritative, Protective.
* **Queen Meritites (The Heart of the Royal Family / Mother):** Early 40s. Wife of Pharaoh Khufu. Elegant and highly respected throughout the royal court. Possesses strong intuition; often understands Neferet better than Khufu does. Historically adds authenticity. *Personality:* Compassionate, Intelligent, Diplomatic, Warm-hearted. *Story Role:* In Book 1, she becomes one of the first people to realize Neferet's feelings for John. In Book 3, her reunion with Neferet could become one of the most emotional scenes in the trilogy.
* **Princess Henutsen (The Younger Sister She Could Never Forget):** Age 15-16. Neferet's younger sister. Playful, energetic, and curious about everything. Often sneaks away from palace lessons. Admires Neferet and tries to imitate her. *Personality:* Cheerful, Mischievous, Loyal, Fearless. *Story Role:* Henutsen is the sister appearing in Neferet's dreams during Book 2. When Neferet disappears through the Gate, Henutsen is devastated. This makes their reunion in Book 3 incredibly powerful.

### Royal Family Tree
```
Pharaoh Khufu  +  Queen Meritites
               │
       ┌───────┴───────┐
Princess Neferet   Princess Henutsen
(Older Daughter)   (Younger Daughter)
```

### Character Dynamics
* **Khufu ↔ Neferet:** Duty vs. freedom.
* **Meritites ↔ Neferet:** Motherly understanding.
* **Henutsen ↔ Neferet:** Best friends as well as sisters.
